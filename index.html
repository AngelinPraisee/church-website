ABOUT OUR CHURCH

NEW LIFE CHURCH

Our Church is a Christ-centered community built on faith, prayer and the unchanging Word of God. Established over 60 years ago, our church stands as a living testimony to God's faithfulness across generations.

What began as a small step of obedience has grown into a spiritual home where believers gather to worship, grow and serve together as one body in Christ. Rooted in Biblical truth, we remain committed to preserving the spiritual foundation laid by our forefathers while continuing to move forward with renewed vision and purpose.

Through every season, God has been our strength, guide and provider.

One-body-in-Christ!

Senior Pastor

Pr. JACOB KOSHY

Pastor K.G. Koshy founded the church in Nov 1 1961. After his death in 1995, Pastor Jacob Koshy is serving as the Senior Pastor of New Life Church, Avadi. He completed his Bible Studies in Elim Bible College, USA. He is a devoted servant of God, who leads the church with faith, humility, and a deep passion for prayer and the word of God. With over 34 years of pastoral Service, he has been shepherding the church with unwavering faith, humility, and a deep commitment to the word of God. Through his leadership, the church has grown spiritually while remaining rooted in its strong foundation and values passed down through generations.
